Privacy Policy

The developer of Degrees does not collect or store any personal data. However, Google Ads, which supplies third-party ads for Degrees through its Admob SDK, may collect some device and/or personal data in accordance with their privacy policy, which can be found here. This data is collected for the purpose of providing more relevant advertisements to the user and can be used to analyze app performance. Data that may be collected by Google while using the app can include:

  • IP Address
  • Non-user related crash logs
  • Device performance data (launch time, energy usage, etc.)
  • Advertising ID or other device identifier
  • Advertisements seen or engaged with
